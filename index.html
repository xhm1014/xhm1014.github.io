Biosketch

Hongming Xu is currently a postdoc fellow in the Department of Quantitative Health Sciences at Learner Research Institute, Cleveland Clinic. He will join Department of Biomedical Engineering at Dalian University of Technology as an associate professor, Dalian, China in January 2021. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Alberta in July 2017, M.S. and Bachelor's degree in College of Inormation Engineering at Northwest A&F University, Yangling China in July 2009 and 2012, respectively. His research focuses on artificial intelligence in biomedical imaging fields (particularly Pathology AI), medical image computing, imaging informatics, and machine learning.


He will look for students who are interested in medical image analysis, deep learning related biomedical applications. He is also interested in research and industrial collaborations regarding to biomedical artificial intelligence. Please feel free to contact him if you are interested.
