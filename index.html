Biosketch

Hongming Xu is currently an Associate Professor in the School of Biomedical Engineering at Faculty of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Dalian University of Technology. He worked as a Postdoc Fellow in Hwang Lab at Cleveland Clinic between Oct. 2017 and Nov.2020. He received his Ph.D. in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Alberta in Edmonton, Canada, July 2017, M.S. and Bachelor's degree in College of Inormation Engineering at Northwest A&F University in Yangling, China, July 2012 and 2009, respectively. His research focuses on artificial intelligence in biomedical imaging fields (particularly Pathology AI), medical image computing, imaging informatics, and machine learning.


He looks for students who are interested in medical image analysis, deep learning related biomedical applications. Students from the Department of Biomedical Engineering as well as other departments (e.g., Computer Science and Engineering, Bioengineering, etc.) are always welcome. He would like to help international students to apply Chinese Scholarships for study in China. He is also interested in research and industrial collaborations regarding to biomedical artificial intelligence. Please feel free to contact him if you are interested.
