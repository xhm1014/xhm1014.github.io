Hongming Xu, Ph.D.

Principal Investigator
Hongming Xu is currently an Associate Professor in the School of Biomedical Engineering at Faculty of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Dalian University of Technology. He received his Ph.D. in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Alberta in Edmonton, Canada, July 2017, M.S. and Bachelor's degree from College of Inormation Engineering at Northwest A&F University in Yangling, China, July 2012 and 2009, respectively. His research focuses on artificial intelligence in biomedical imaging fields (particularly Pathology AI), medical image computing, imaging informatics, and machine learning.




Xinyu, Hao Ph.D. Candidate

Research Assistant
Xinyu Hao will join University of Jyvaskyla, Finland, as a PhD student under the support of Chinese Scholarship Council (CSC). His research focuses on machine learning and deep learning. His research projects involve the development and application of mathematical, computational, and statistical techniques to theoretical and methodological problems within the areas of computational pathology, quantitative methodology, and biomedical image analysis. he is also working with Prof. Fengyu Cong.




Shan,Jin, Ph.D. Candidate

Research Assistant
Shan Jin is a PhD candidate and a research assistant in the School of Biomedical Engineering, Faculty of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Dalian University of Technology. She works on computational radiology and pathology projects that have included applications of machine learning and computer vision algorithms for clinical diagnosis and outcome predictions. She is also working with Prof. Fengyu Cong.
